About me

My name is Faijan Ahamed (born on 30 march 2003). I am a student. Curently, I am pursuing bachelor's degree in the department of computer science and engineering from University of Lucknow which is located in Lucknow (The capital of Uttarpradesh). Lucknow University is a very prestigious government university of India.

My journey to the University from School life is priceless. I have completed my 10th standard from my village Sikariganj in 2019. I have scored highest marks in my school securing 94.8% from CBSE Board. I have moved to city Gorakhpur in class 11th which is the district of Sikariganj.

I started preparing for IIT JEE exam in class 11th. I have taken admission in Govt. Jubilee Inter College in class 11th and also I have joined coaching in the best institute of gorakhpur. I have prepared for 2 years. I have cleared mains but could not qualify advanced. I had decided to take 1 year gap and moved to Kota rajasthan and taken admission in Motion Education for IIT JEE preparation. In this year I have scored more marks than the previous year in Mains. Due to my bad heath condition I could not able to qualify advanced this year also. On the basis of JEE mains scorecard I have got admission in Lucknow University in CSE department. I have been recovered from my sickness and now I am happy because despite being sick I have been admitted in the best university of my state.
